Contemporary Dance Class by Eva Klimackova (France/ Slovakia)

Contemporary Dance Class

I propose technical work in a very playful and sensorial way, inspired by Body Mind Centering. I am looking for a soft quality of movement and release attach to the work of muscles. To encourage the suspension of the body I use the weight and the principle of spiral. Altering diverse possibilities of body coordination I use a lot of upside-down and spine movements. Altering speed from very slow (process of consciousness) until very fast I encourage to take a risk and to trust the body, his instinct and his pleasure of dancing.

Eva Klimackova is dancer, choreographer and teacher born in Slovakia, studied at the Academy of performing Arts in Bratislava. Afterwards she moved to Paris, where she lives since 2001. She participated in divers projects in France (Kubilai Khan Investigations, Laurent Goldring, Faustin Linyekula, co Petite Fabrique...), Belgium (co. Dame de Pic – Karine Ponties, David Hernandez, Lise Duclaux), Slovakia (Anna Sedlackova, Studio tanca, Milan Kozanek, Kata Mojzisova, Marta Polakova...), Czech Republic (co. Dejadonné, co. Duwadance)... Eva teaches regularly in Paris, Brussels, Prague, Bratislava, Brno, Orléans, Grenoble, Limoges, La Rochelle, Poitiers, Tours, Hanoi. In 2007 she founded the company E7KA in Paris and created performances: “Alzbeta Hlucha” (2007), “Alzbeta” (2008), “Ivanuska” (2009), “Touch.ed” (2011), “Move” (2013) and “The perception off” (2016), presented in France, Slovakia, Czech republic, Slovenia, Germany and Hong Kong.
